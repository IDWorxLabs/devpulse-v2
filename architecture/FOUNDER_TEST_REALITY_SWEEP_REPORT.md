## Reality Sweep Categories

1. **Execution Reality** — Can the system actually build software?
2. **Founder Experience** — Can a founder understand what to do?
3. **First-Time User Experience** — Would a new user be confused?
4. **Navigation Reality** — Can users find important functionality?
5. **Live Preview Reality** — Can users actually view what was built?
6. **Verification Reality** — Can users trust the results?
7. **AI Interaction Reality** — Does AiDev understand requests correctly?
8. **Missing Capability Reality** — What major capabilities are absent?
9. **Launch Risk Reality** — What would stop adoption?
10. **Competitive Reality** — What capabilities are expected before launch?
