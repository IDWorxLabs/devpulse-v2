## Consistency rules

| Rule | Flag | When |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | `CONSISTENCY_FAILURE` | Different verdicts from same evidence class |
| 2 | `SCORING_DEFECT` | Score contradicts scenario outcomes (e.g. 0/100 + all passed) |
| 3 | `EVIDENCE_PROPAGATION_FAILURE` | Evidence exists but downstream authority did not consume it |
| 4 | `AUTHORITY_DISAGREEMENT` | Authorities evaluate different questions |
| 5 | `REAL_PRODUCT_GAP` | Evidence genuinely missing |
