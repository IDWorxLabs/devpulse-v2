# Revenue Reality Authority

**Phase 26.17** — Read-only authority evaluating whether a product is generating real economic value.

## Core Question

Is this product creating measurable business value?

Adoption answers whether users depend on the product. Revenue reality answers whether value is being exchanged.

## Architecture

```
Adoption Reality Authority
Post-Launch Reality Authority
Founder Launch Decision Authority
Billing / Subscription / Accounting evidence
  → Revenue Evidence Analyzer
  → Customer Value Analyzer
  → Conversion Analyzer
  → Revenue Stability Analyzer
  → Business Risk Analyzer
  → Revenue Verdict Engine
  → Revenue Reality Report
```

## Revenue States

| State | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| NO_REVENUE | No observed payment or transaction evidence |
| EARLY_REVENUE | Initial revenue exchange detected |
| REPEAT_REVENUE | Repeat purchases or returning paying customers |
| SUSTAINABLE_REVENUE | Recurring and consistent revenue patterns |
| BUSINESS_ENGINE | Predictable recurring revenue engine |

## Strict Rules

- Users ≠ revenue
- Adoption ≠ revenue
- Traffic, signups, interest, and intent ≠ revenue
- Claims ≠ revenue

## Safety

- No billing actions, payment processing, or revenue creation
- No synthetic revenue, estimated revenue, or invented customers
- Absence of evidence remains absence of evidence

## Future Chain

Idea → Plan → Build → Validate → Runtime → Launch Ready → Launch Decision → Post-Launch Reality → Adoption Reality → **Revenue Reality** → Product Evolution Reality
